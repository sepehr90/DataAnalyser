Background

Sebastian is a skillful and dedicated software developer with more than 8 years of education and work in the Information Technology sector. Sebastian has an extensive, strong and wide range of experience and knowledge in software development methodologies e.g. Scrum, analysing, development using various technologies and frameworks such as .NET, Play and Spring, documenting, coding with numerous programming languages such as C# and Java, modifying, testing and implementing software systems for different industry sectors such as finance and telecommunication e.g. Ericsson. Alongside Sebastian’s software development experiences, his scientific publications about Software requirements and quality in international conferences stands for his expertise in the field of Information technology. Sebastian is a self-directed, goal oriented software developer with remarkable sense of responsibility. Working and studying in different countries, with different cultures, has made him an articulate and fluent communicator, flexible and responsive.
